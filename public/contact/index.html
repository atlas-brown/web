Research with us #

Research is fun — especially when it’s collaborative. Joining the group depends on your status and goal:

  • Current Brown students, students interested in a research visit, or students intersted in remote collaborations: fill out this form.
  • Prospective undergraduate or M.Sc. students: do not email the group — we are not involved in the admissions process.
  • Prospective Ph.D. students: apply directly to the CS department’s Ph.D. program, mentioning Prof. Vasilakis.
  • Prospective postdocs: email Prof. Vasilakis with your CV, two representative papers, and a description of your background and interests.
